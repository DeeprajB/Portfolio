About

Myself

Hey! My name is Deepraj Bhosale. I'm 19 years old. I ventured into the world of photography in 2016, and it has been my passion ever since. My main domain is event based photography, but I've experimented on candid and creative photography. Like most photographers, my journey started out with just a phone in my hand, but a whole world of possibilities to explore. My phone came with a 'pro-mode' which let me choose the 'ISO' ,'shutter speed', 'focus', 'white balance' etc. I found myself fascinated with what I could capture through the manual approach. Over the years, I've invested my time learning, experimenting, and exploring whatever I could. Apart from photography, I've also been learning various cinematography techniques since 2019. One of the areas that intrigued me was creating time lapses and hyperlapses. There is a certain beauty about timelapses created of nature. It redefines the art of storytelling, allowing us to appreciate the wonders hidden in simple everyday moments. My other interests include web development and game development. I've created sites to enhance and showcase my web development skill. Game development is a field I'm currently exploring. So far, I've created a few mini games which were fun to work on. Thank You.
